    Zorro Finance

    Zorro Finance is a Hong Kong based debt re-structuring firm, catering to the needs of Real Estate companies, single family offices and small institutions. Zorro Finance is independently owned, free of any conflict-of-interest firm that firm that provides bespoke solutions to its clients. With its vast network, deep expertise and close attention to detail, our firm is focused to assisting each client achieve its specific requirements.

    Zorro Finance is 70% owned by its management and 30% owned by external investors who add value through liquidity financing under repo structures.

    The core team of Zorro Finance consists of individuals with over 75 years combined experience in debt financing, mezzanine structuring and general investment banking.

    We are able to draw on extensive debt finance capability across the wide-ranging network, as well as the firm's legal, advisory and real estate teams, to assist our clients with structuring and executing their transactions.

    • Tailored Services

    Our services are designed to take a holistic view on our client’s financing requirements. When things go wrong, we are able to provide guidance on the re-structuring process of transactions and exit strategies through our restructuring and insolvency team.

    • Strategic Asset Allocation: We are able to provide advice on how a client’s debt book should be managed.
    • Core Strategies: Understanding and implementing which Core Strategies that should be part of the heart of the business model.
    • Specific Tactical Trades: Identifying specific isolated debt investments, which compliment the Strategic Asset Allocation.
    • Risk Management: We both set up, monitor and carefully identify any risks that may derive from the investment decisions of the client.
    • Advisory Portfolio

    Depending on the client’s investment goals, appetite for risk, style etc., Zorro Finance offers different types of deal tickets to meet the requirements of its clients:

    • Risk-Profile Mandates: We use our own internal risk matrix to gage the client's risk tolerance.
    • Tailor-made Mandates: We use specialist systems to both analyse and understand our client’s needs
    • Thematic Mandates: We cater for all geographical locations and capital protection structures.
